About the CHS Computer Science Club

The Computer Science Club provides a central hub for people of all interests in the spectrum of Computer Science, such as algorithmic knowledge, Computer Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Idea of Things, blockchain technology, and more!

While we do primarily focus on the algorithmic spectrum, club members are more than welcome to link up with those who are like-minded to themselves in their interests.
